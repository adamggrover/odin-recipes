Beef Pie

Description

A rich beef pie recipe perfect for those cold nights. Especially good washed down with a pint of Ale.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Ale
  • Carrots
  • Beef shin
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt

Method

  1. Make pastry
  2. Roll out and line pie dish. Cut lid/li>
  3. Make pie filling. Put in pastry and put lid on top. Egg wash
  4. Put in oven till cooked
  5. Eat
