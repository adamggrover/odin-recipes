Victoria Sponge

Description

A proper Victoria Sponge just like Nan used to make. Knock this up and sit down with a gurt cup of tea. Lovely stuff!

Ingredients

  • Caster sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Cream
  • Strawberries
  • Butter
  • Icing sugar

Method

  1. Beat butter and sugar together till pale
  2. Add eggs one by one
  3. Fold in flour and baking powder
  4. Put in oven till cooked
  5. Leave to cool
  6. Whip cream
  7. Put layer of jam and cream in middle
  8. Decorate with strawberries and icing sugar
  9. Eat
